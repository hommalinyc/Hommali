Hommali is a love letter to Thailand—shaped by memory and distance.
Being away made those flavors mean more, and this is how we share them.
We wanted the space to feel soulful, a little chaotic, and playful—warm, unpolished, and alive.
A place to step out of the everyday.
We cook like jazz—starting familiar, then improvising with instinct and memory.
Some dishes feel like home, others surprise, all rooted in nostalgia and creativity.
It’s about honoring where we come from while letting it evolve into something expressive and full of soul.
